PIAIC

Presidential Iniative for Artificial Intellegence and Cloud Computing

PIAIC (Pakistan's Artificial Intelligence and Computing) is a non-profit educational organization that aims to transform Pakistan into a global hub for Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, and other cutting-edge technologies. The organization's mission is to provide accessible and affordable AI and computing education to people from all walks of life, regardless of their socioeconomic background, gender, or age.
